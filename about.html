About

Tropicwathna Sun.

Creative, image-maker, and the mind behind Yato's Batcave — a studio where everything is treated as art, and nothing is left in full color until it earns it.

I work across the space between documentary and dream: visuals that sit quietly between memory and moment, stripped of color until you lean in close enough to remember they had any. Each frame begins in monochrome and earns its color through attention.

This gallery is a living archive — a rotating collection of stills, experiments, and personal work. The thread through all of it is restraint: knowing what to leave out so the one thing that matters can breathe.
